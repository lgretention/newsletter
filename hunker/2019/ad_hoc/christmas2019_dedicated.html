MAKE A MODERN WREATH
This wreath is an easy (and gorgeous) way to add a whimsical, homemade touch to your home this holiday season.
DIY IT

Step One: First, take your paddle wire and cut a nice long piece (about one foot) and set it aside.

Step Two: Next, cut your stems down into smaller pieces and gather them to your liking.

Step Three: Take your wire and bind the stems together. Continue making bundles and layering them.

Step Four: Create one more small bundle, flip it the other direction, and tuck it inside to cover the stem ends.

Step Five: Finally, take some of your favorite ribbon, create a loop, and secure it at the top of your wreath.

Step Six: Place on your door! With your extra greenery, feel free to make more wreaths — they make great gifts.
